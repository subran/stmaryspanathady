Committee for SC/ST

St. MARY’S college constituted a Committee for SC/ST to support students in the reserved category and provides special inputs in areas where the students experience difficulties.

The committee conducts regular remedial coaching classes on life skills, personality development, writing assignments, and making presentations, as well as English and local language classes. The Committee also organizes interactive sessions and informal meetings with students to attend to their personal, social, and academic problems.

The college takes special interest in facilitating financial support to students from these communities from government agencies and other sources. The cell is always engaged to solve the various problems of SC/ST students.
